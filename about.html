Our Mission

Badger Future Founders is a student-run entrepreneurship community at UW-Madison. We exist to help students explore startup ideas, connect with mentors, and build the skills they need to launch real ventures — regardless of major or experience level.

What We Do

Workshops

Hands-on sessions covering topics like pitching, product development, and fundraising basics.

Networking

Opportunities to meet local founders, alumni entrepreneurs, and fellow students who share your drive.

Pitch Events

Showcase your ideas in front of judges, get feedback, and compete for recognition on campus.

Get Involved

Whether you have a startup idea or just want to learn, there is a place for you at BFF.
